### 1. Start a Group Order (Host)
1.  **Login** to your account (required to create a group).
2.  Click the **Users Icon** (ðŸ‘¥) in the top navigation bar, or go to `/group-order`.
3.  (Optional) Set a **Deadline** for when the order should be finalized.
4.  Click **"Create Group Order"**.
5.  You will be redirected to a unique Group Order page (e.g., `/group-order/a1b2c3d4`).

### 2. Invite Friends
1.  Copy the **URL** from your browser address bar.
2.  Share this link with your friends via WhatsApp, Slack, or Email.

### 3. Join a Group Order (Participants)
1.  Open the shared link.
2.  Enter your **Name** (if you are not logged in).
3.  Click **"Join Order"**.

### 4. Add Items
1.  Browse the **Menu** on the left side of the screen.
2.  Click **"Add to Order"** on any item.
3.  The item will instantly appear in the **Group Cart** on the right side.
4.  Everyone in the group will see the item added in real-time!

### 5. Finalize & Pay
1.  Once everyone has added their items, the Host can review the **Group Cart**.
2.  Click **"Proceed to Checkout"** to place the order for everyone.
